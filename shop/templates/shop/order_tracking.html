Where to find your tracking code?

Your tracking code is a unique identifier sent to your email after placing an order. Check your order confirmation email for a UUID format code.

Order Status Meanings

Pending:
Your order has been received and is being processed.
Processing:
Your order is being prepared for shipment.
Shipped:
Your order has been shipped and is on its way.
Delivered:
Your order has been delivered to the shipping address.
